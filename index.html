Specialists In Our Field

We specialise in complex mortgage cases, including proving income or low income, self-employment, and adverse or low credit scoreapplications.

Based in the city of London, for over 25 years we have helped clients all over the UK who have been refused by their bank or high street lender to obtain mortgages. We help clients on a daily basis who struggle to raise funding, whether that be for a purchase – re-mortgage – buy to let -commercial or first-time buyers. We pride ourselves on getting results for clients who face difficulties obtaining a mortgage in today’s market.

We offer free, no obligation, expert comprehensive advice – every time. We are totally independent and have access to the whole of the UK lending market, including private funders who purely focus and specialise in low income and adverse lending.

Here at Direct Mortgages, we fully appreciate how difficult and daunting this process can be. The anxiety of waiting for lenders to say ‘yes’, and then the frustration of being refused a mortgage time and time again. We can help even if you have been turned down elsewhere!

Lean on our expertise and take advantage of our vast knowledge and years of funding contacts. You are not alone, we will provide you with the guidance required to see your case through fully from start to finish, and can even arrange highly experienced conveyancing solicitors to represent you.

At Direct Mortgages, we guarantee from the moment we receive your instruction of appointment, we will be relentless in the quest to get you the ‘yes’ acceptance you need and your official mortgage offer. We do not use comparable websites, we are direct with banks and funders, with the latest software enabling us to get the very best interest rate deals available in the UK at a touch of a button.

If you need our assistance in getting funding we are more than happy to help. We are available 7 days a week (Saturdays & Sundays 11am – 5pm).
